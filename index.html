About Kithsiri Athulathmudali

Kithsiri (Keith) Athulathmudali is a distinguished leader known for his expertise in international business, strategy, and diplomatic service. His career, which includes extensive time in the United States and serving as the Ambassador-designate of Sri Lanka to the State of Qatar, is a testament to his dedication to service and creating long-term value. He is driven by a philosophy of **"thinking beyond the obvious"** and is a keen aviator.

Professional Trajectory

Ambassador to Qatar (Designate)

Embassy of Sri Lanka, Doha | 2019

Tasked with significantly strengthening bilateral ties, focusing efforts on enhancing **trade and foreign investment**. Advocated for the welfare of the large Sri Lankan expatriate community in Qatar, including initiatives to improve consular services and educational facilities.

Managing Director, Asia Pacific

Honeywell Inc. | Based in Thailand & Singapore

Spearheaded the expansion of a global business unit across 35 countries in the Asia-Pacific region. Instrumental in driving strategy and sales growth, leveraging his engineering and business acumen to achieve market dominance.

Founder & Investor

Multiple Ventures (e.g., EggSystems, Healthcare)

Founded **EggSystems Pte Ltd**, focusing on building management systems, and received the **'Entrepreneur of the Year' (2001) in Singapore**. Also established and later sold several successful specialized **Alzheimer's care facilities** in Dallas, Texas.

Director, Strategic Planning

Kaiser Foundation Hospitals / Johnson Controls

Held senior management roles early in his career, including a **Director position at Kaiser Foundation Hospitals**. His work involved strategic planning and managing complex international operations across multiple countries.
